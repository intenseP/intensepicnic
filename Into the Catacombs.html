Into the Catacombs

Last updated: 22/04/24


Overview

Into the Catacombs is a roguelite, a game where you traverse the Catacombs and Dungeons crawling with creatures. Purge the cellars of these critters and your gods will reward you. Spill more blood, aquire abilities and items to assist you against what lies at the end of the tunnels...

Controls

WASD for movement. Click to attack, right-click to block, shift to roll in the direction you are looking and 'Q', 'E' and 'V' to use abilty slots 1, 2 and 3 respectively. To pause, press Space or the icon in the top-left.

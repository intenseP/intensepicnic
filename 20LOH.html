20 Levels of Hell

Last updated: 28/02/24


Overview

20 Levels of Hell is a simple platformer and the adventures of a black square. Traverse the 25 levels of increasing difficulty, getting new abilties along the way. It may start off easy, but I can assure you, you will NOT be completing it. Level 25 is still not confirmed to be possible.

Controls

By default, moving is controlled by A to move left, D to move right. W is the jump key. R will restart from the last Checkpoint. To slide, hold shift while moving to slide in that direction. To pause, press Space. Default keybinds for each action is displayed when beginning and all settings can be adjusted to your liking in the 'Options' tab.

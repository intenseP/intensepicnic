  • Into The Catacombs Patch v1.15

    Released: 27/04/24

    Into The Catacombs v1.15 Patch Notes:

    • Treasury Guardian is now able to be spawned and fought in the battle arena

    • Boss takes 1 less second to teleport to the indicated position (was: 2.25, is now 1.25)

    • Souls paid starts from zero everytime the shrine is activated, ensuring the value is not at an illegal value

    • 2 easter eggs have been added

    • Slowness duration decreased from 7.5 to 4 seconds

    • Poison duration increased from 7.5 to 10 seconds

    • Explosions now blow up nearby explosives and side assets such as barrels, crates and chests (loot not lost)

    • Marginal increase in chance of getting health from side assets

    • Assets are broken in the same manner as a mob is attacked

    • Cauldrons have a 50-50 chance to have negative or positive effects

    • Cauldron +health has been reduced from 20 to 10 hp

    • UI redesigns

    • Sorcerers are unable to wave attack when you are right next to them

    • Digger Changes (Buff):

      • Burrow cooldown reduced from 3 to 1 second

      • Time taken for Digger to burrow (become invincible) reduced from 0.75 to 0.5 seconds

      • Speed increased from 5 to 6

      • Attack cooldown reduced from 3 to 1 seconds

      • Attack stuns for 0.5 seconds (was 0.1 second)

      • Diggers have a small self-heal while underground for over 1.5 seconds

      • Health increased from 20 to 25

    • Time taken for the shield to activate decreased from 0.5 to 0.25 seconds after the right mouse button clicked

    • Mobs (that drop health) have a 5% larger chance to yield health upon death

    • Camera Shake (when attacking) option to toggle added

    • Player roll distance nerfed by 40%
